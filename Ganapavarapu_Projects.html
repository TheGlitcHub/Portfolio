Scratch Project

The Water & Fire Platformer Game was a program made for time pass or for when someone is bored. The game is about a water spirit trying to get to its home on the beach, but it is stuck in a Volcano. In each of the three total levels, the user must make the sprite get a magical key to get to the next level, and from there the key transforms into a wizard to help you get out of the volcano and complete the game.
